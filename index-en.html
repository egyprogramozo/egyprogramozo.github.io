Introduction

Gergely Hajdu

Location: Budapest
Occupation: Computer Science Engineer

About Me

I have over 15 years of professional experience in IT operations and data warehouse development, where I gained deep insight into and supported the operation of complex systems within large enterprise environments. I have always prioritized uninterrupted service and robust security, and in recent years I have increasingly oriented my career toward cybersecurity.

Read more

To support this transition, I have pursued targeted training to expand and develop my expertise, ensuring I remain up-to-date with practical skills in this fast-evolving field. I am particularly passionate about incident response, threat analysis, and continuously improving the state of system security - areas where I can use my strong analytical mindset and problem-solving abilities.

As an open and eager learner, I believe in contributing to the success of my team not only through technical expertise but also with a collaborative, reliable attitude. I’m convinced that continuous growth, responsible thinking, and meticulous attention to detail are indispensable in cybersecurity, and I strive to create value in every task I undertake.
I would be delighted to join a team where I can further develop my skills, gain new experience, and help drive the organization’s security objectives forward.

What Motivates Me?

I am driven primarily by opportunities for professional growth - such as specialized courses, challenging projects, and hands-on training - as well as a positive work environment and both financial and ethical recognition. It’s crucial for me to work on assignments that deliver tangible value and allow me to fully leverage my expertise.

Professional Interests

From the outset, I’ve been fascinated by emerging technologies. I have always been open to new developments, striving to stay up-to-date with industry innovations. My career has spanned web development, database management, and operations, where I also handled help-desk style user support. In recent years, I’ve shifted my focus to cybersecurity to take an active role in defending systems.

I believe the breadth of my technical background provides a solid foundation for effectively tackling cybersecurity challenges.
